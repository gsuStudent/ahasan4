Welcome to the Game of 30. The simple, yet fun and exciitng game geared towards young adults. The concept of this game is very simple to understand. Gain 30 points by drawing various numerical cards and you win the game! However, draw the wrong cards, and earn more than 30 points and you will be faced with a loss. This game is fast paced, and can be played on the go. Play with your friends, family or even by yourself. Want a challenge? Go against our custom built AI. Now that you have come this far, what are you waiting for? Let the festivities begin with the Game of 30! To find out more, please explore the links above.

The Game of 30 has a simple and easy to read interface. When you open the game itself, you are greeted by a gorgeous menu screen. The menu consists of 3 easy to understand buttons. Each card in the menu will display vibrant colors. The theme of this menu was created with the intent of displaying beautiful asthetiscs that are pleasing to the eyes. As you can see the Game of 30 does not disappoint!
