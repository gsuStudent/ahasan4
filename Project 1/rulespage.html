The Rules of 30

  • King : Immune to Joker
  • Queen : Return your highest valued card to the deck
  • Jack : Return all of the cards you control to the deck
  • Ace : Must Draw 3 Cards
  • Joker : You Lose!

Win condition = 2 + 5 + 8 + 4 + 6 + 5 = win !! If total summation of numerical cards > 30, you lose
